Our Family Story

From our family groves in Kalamata, Greece, Ana’s Estate Extra Virgin Olive Oil is produced exclusively from hand-harvested Koroneiki olives.

The olives are cold extracted within hours of harvest to preserve their exceptional flavour, aroma and natural quality.

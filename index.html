Our Heritage

Our Family Story

◇

From our family groves in Kalamata, Greece, Ana’s Estate Extra Virgin Olive Oil is produced exclusively from carefully selected Koroneiki olives.

Our olives are harvested with care and cold extracted within hours of harvest to preserve their exceptional flavour, fresh aroma and natural quality.

Every bottle reflects our family’s connection to the land, our Greek heritage and our commitment to producing authentic Kalamata olive oil.

From Grove to Bottle

Our Harvest Process

◇

Each stage is carefully managed to protect the fruit and preserve the flavour, aroma and natural quality of the finished oil.

01

Early Harvest

Olives are gathered during the early harvest period to capture freshness, aroma and natural intensity.

02

Carefully Gathered

The fruit is handled carefully to protect the olives and preserve the quality of the oil they produce.

03

Cold Extracted

Extraction takes place within hours of harvest at a controlled temperature to preserve flavour and freshness.

04

Protected and Packed

The finished oil is protected from heat and light before being prepared for its journey to Canada.
